About Us

We want to create unique outdoor events that raise awareness and funds for social issues in Ireland. Our events will use creativity and imagination to inspire young people to take action and make a change for the better. Together we can change the world. Our core values are to be consistent, relevant and compassionate. Taking inspiration from Irish hip hop band, Versatile, we came up with a theme that ties in with our mission statement and core values.
